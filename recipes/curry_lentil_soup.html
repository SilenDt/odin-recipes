curry lentil soup

Description:

A pumkin coconut curry lentil soup, perfected over many years, by the great chef Eilidh. This soup is fairly easy to make, and doesnt require too many crazy ingredients. Cooking time will vary, depending on what type of kitchen tools you are working with.

This dish is also very healthy, and will please any mouth it feeds.

Ingredients

  • coconut oil
  • 1 tablespoon red curry paste
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 2 cups red lentils
  • 4 cups water or broth (chicken or veggie)
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1/2 can of coconut milk
  • 1 teaspoon tumeric
  • Optional: add fresh roasted pumpkin or butternut sqash. (can of pureed pumpkin also works)

Instructions

  1. Wash lentils (soaking is optional)
  2. sautee onions, garlic, and ginger in coconut oil. add salt and pepper here also.
  3. add lentils to onion/garlic/ginger and make sure they
  4. add curry powder and tumeric
  5. bring water/broth to a boil and add lentil mixture
  6. mix in red curry paste
  7. reduce to a low simmer
  8. cook until lentils are soft (about an hour or so)
  9. add coconut milk and stir
  10. serve and enjoy!
